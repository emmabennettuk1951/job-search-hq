The full loop

Gmail (Google Alert + LinkedIn emails) → Zapier → Google Sheet (published as CSV) → this dashboard's Inbox. Once live, hit Sync in the Inbox tab.

Gmail trigger — search string

Use this combined string to catch both Google Alerts and LinkedIn job emails:

from:googlealerts-noreply@google.com OR from:jobs-noreply@linkedin.com
